These are the (placeholder) requirements every solution design is assessed against.

## Capability Alignment
- The solution MUST map to a named business capability or strategic objective.

## Stakeholder Mapping
- Sponsor and primary stakeholders MUST be identified, with their interest stated.

## Business Case & Value
- Expected benefits MUST be stated, ideally with measurable target KPIs/OKRs.
- A cost-benefit or ROI consideration SHOULD be included.

## Business Risk
- Material business risks (adoption, dependency, compliance) SHOULD be documented.

## Operational Impact
- Operational ownership, SLAs, and support model SHOULD be described.
- Impact on existing processes SHOULD be noted.
